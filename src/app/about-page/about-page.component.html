About Me

I’m Jess. I’ve been traveling and writing about it since 2011. I’ve had a lot of unusual adventures, so I have a lot of things to write about.


I’ve danced up and down the white beaches of the Bahamas dressed in a banana suit. I spent a summer Couchsurfing around Europe. I’ve stayed in hostels all over the world, made countless new friends, slept on airport floors, and taken more overnight buses than I can count. I also know a lot of travel tips and tricks that I love to share with my readers. My writing has appeared in more than 30 publications, most notably the Huffington Post and Fodor’s Travel Guide!


Since 2014, I have also been writing about dating and relationships since 2014. I wrote a weekly dating column for a year, and consequently am not only familiar with most of the dating apps and sites that currently exist, but have also personally interviewed the founder and/or CEO of almost every dating site or app that came out of the San Francisco Bay Area in that time. (Trust me, there are a lot.)


I also ended up working for one of the dating apps whose founders I interviewed, so I know how dating apps work from the other side as well. Since 2017, I have been using my experience in the dating app industry to edit dating apps for clients, using my experience.


I hope my travel tales and tips help inspire your next adventure - maybe with a new partner by your side.


Love,


Jess.
